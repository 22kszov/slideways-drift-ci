Slide like a pro!

Drifting is a highly competitive and intense sport wordlwide full of high powered cars, smell of burning rubber and thrilling sideways action.

Altough this is true at the competitive level there is nothing stopping the average person at home getting out on the track in their budget build shredding tyres and still having just as much fun!

Here at Slideways we will introduce you to everything you need for getting started on your own drifting journey.

We will help you pick a car and go over the modifications you need to get it track ready so you can get yourself Slideways.
