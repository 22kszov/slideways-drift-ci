Pick your car!

A lot of people simply don't know where to look when trying to pick a car to slide. Most beginners assume you need a $40,000 car when this isn't the case at all.

Drifting on a budget definitely has it's challenges but it is more than doable. When looking for a car to learn in all you really need is a front-engine, rear wheel drive(RWD) car with decent power around 200-300hp. Still unsure? Here is our top pick for you.

Nissan 350z

The 350z is a two-door RWD 6 speed manual sports car. It is a perfect entry level machine not just for it's pretty curves but also for it's performance.

This little toy comes equipped with a 3.5l, v6 pushing 301 horsepower. The car comes with an lsd from factory and a front mid-engine layout leaving the car with good weight distribution.

If that's not enough you can also pick one of these cars up for $5-8,000 making it budget friendly as well!

Now that you have picked your car it's time for the fun stuff, spending your hard earned cash on some mods to get it track ready!
